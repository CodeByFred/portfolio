About Me

My career journey has been defined by an interest in understanding people and a drive for technological innovation. I began my university career in the United States with a psychology degree because I have always been intrigued by human behaviour. This foundation honed my communication skills and insight into people but also laid the groundwork for a unique perspective that I bring to technology.

After moving to Australia, I wanted to continue my education and expand my interests. I enrolled in an electrical engineering program. It was during this time that a programming course gave me a passion that redefined my career path, leading me to pursue an honours degree in software engineering. Today, I combine my insights into human behaviour with technical expertise to create innovative solutions that drive meaningful change.
