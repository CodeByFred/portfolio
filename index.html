About Me

My career journey started with an interest in human behaviour, which led me to pursue a psychology degree in the United States. That foundation sharpened my communication skills and gave me a unique lens for understanding people — a perspective I now bring into tech.

After moving to Australia, I studied electrical engineering to broaden my interests. It was during a programming course that everything clicked. That spark turned into a full-blown passion, and I shifted gears to pursue an honours degree in software engineering.

Now, I blend behavioural insight with technical skills to build thoughtful, human-centered solutions that aim to make a difference.
